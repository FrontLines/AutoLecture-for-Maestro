Welcome to the AutoLecture for Maestro Installer!

AutoLecture is an intelligent, automated text-to-speech companion designed specifically for Maestro learning platforms.

Key Features:
- Fully Local TTS Engine: Your voice generation happens directly on your machine, ensuring complete privacy and fast generation.
- Dynamic Bubble Reading: AutoLecture intelligently tracks your progress and automatically reads AI chat bubbles and lesson content.
- Code Block Reading: Seamlessly extracts and reads code snippets generated within AI Teacher Prompts.
- Native Chrome Integration: Manage playback, pause, and skip effortlessly from the Chrome Extension popup.

Data Privacy Guarantee:
AutoLecture operates entirely in real-time. It NEVER saves, downloads, or logs your lesson prompts, chat history, or generated audio files. Once the audio is spoken, it is immediately discarded from system memory.

This setup wizard will guide you through the installation of both the Local Voice Server and the Chrome Extension files.
